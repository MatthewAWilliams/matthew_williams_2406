19.5 problems that would be solved or improved:
-Inventory would be updated almost immediately, so less time would be spent counting by hand.
-Once a transaction is completed, the inventory will automatically update itself.
-Shipments to and from the different sites would be added or substracted from the inventory just by entering a set of numbers.
-The inventory will also be updated once an online order is completed.
-Errors can easily be corrected, from the supplier or on site, by simply going into the system and manualing correcting it. 

20.Sytem Improvement objectives:
-Reduce time taken to count inventory by 90%
-No more updating inventory manually after transactions.
-No more updating inventory manually after shipping or receiving products.
-No more updating inventory manually after an online order is placed.
-Any errors in count of inventory can be manually entered into system.
